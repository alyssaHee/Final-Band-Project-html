My Favourite Performance

My favourite performance was the 2023 wind ensemble festival performance. I think our band really came together that year and our Symphonic Songs for Band “air band” rehearsal before the performance made it really special. I felt like we were all united and focused that hour and the performance felt amazing. I was very proud of us and I remember students from Maples Collegiate sitting in the first few rows taking notes on our playing and that made me more proud.

My second favourite performance would have to be the last concert where we played Symphonic Dance no.3 ‘Fiesta’. I’ve never heard/felt the symphonic band sound so together and strong; the performance was like magic.
